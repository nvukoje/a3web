GIS Databases and Remote Sensing Image Analysis

Data management services within GIS, and acquiring said data from Remote Sensing

We specialize in data management services where our work within ArcGIS and the attribute tables therein can be used to provide insight to decision making. Through use of Structured Query Language (SQL) we can construct geodatabases that store a vast trove of information. The insight from these databases are integrated into our map making process that better informs decision makers. We can also provide land cover maps through remote sensing through use of the electro-magenetic spectrum (EMS) and raster classification. With imagery collected through different wavelengths of the EMS, we can in turn classify the pixelated image to highlight different land use through a region.

Surveying, CAD Mapping and Geodesy

We acquire spatial and attribute data, in the field, and efficiently integrate new data back in the office. Leveraging the skill sets within the consultancy, associated with measuring for maps and land-type surveys, we place field data into existing maps and plans. These collected features are then placed in a GIS/Land Information System and appended to existing digital maps and plans. We use the science related to the determination of the size and shape of the earth. Due to the curvature of the earth, geodetic principles are applied to small scale maps covering large areas.
